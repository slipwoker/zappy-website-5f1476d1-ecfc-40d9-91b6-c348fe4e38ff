Common questions

What information should I bring?

Please bring your device, any relevant accessories (chargers, cables), and a description of the issue you're experiencing. If you have warranty information or previous repair records, those are helpful too.

How do you confirm the issue?

Our computer repair technicians perform a comprehensive diagnostic test using professional-grade tools. We'll examine hardware, software, and connections to identify the exact problem before recommending any solution.

What if repair is not recommended?

If the repair cost exceeds the device's value or if replacement is more practical, we'll provide honest recommendations. We can also help you with data backup and transfer services to preserve your important files.

How do you protect my device during service?

We use ESD-safe workstations, anti-static tools, and clean room protocols. Your device is handled with care at every step, and we maintain detailed tracking throughout the entire repair process for complete accountability.

Do you offer warranty on repairs?

Yes, all repairs come with a warranty covering parts and labor. The specific duration depends on the type of repair performed, and we'll provide full warranty details before you approve any work.

Can you repair devices from all brands?

We service a wide range of electronics including computers, laptops, tablets, and mobile devices from major manufacturers. If you have a specific brand or model, contact us to confirm compatibility.
